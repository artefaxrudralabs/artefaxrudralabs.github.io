Problem

Billions of ethereally beautiful images are created every week by humans and AI. Virtually none of these manifest into the real world of physical merchandise. Creating, editing, enhancing and printing AI-Merch easily and simply for the consumer is a challenge.

We will change that. Our suite of products and technologies will accelerate the proliferation of AI Inspired art onto real world merch.
